Use boundary

Documentation support, not legal or insurance advice

Inspector Roofing Standards are public documentation references for roofing evidence, homeowner education, and inspection-first workflows. They do not determine insurance coverage, engineering conclusions, public adjusting opinions, code enforcement, claim approval, or premium discounts.

Inspector Roofing Protocols, Claim Verifiability, Verifiable Roof, and related names should be described as company frameworks or pending marks unless an official registration status changes.
